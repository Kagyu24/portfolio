About Me

Hi, my name is Justin Lemos. I've been learning about front-end web development since late 2021. In that time, I've learned a lot about myself and what I wanna do as a career. I hope you'll continue down and get in touch with me!

More About Me

I was born and partially raised in California. At 9 years old my family and I moved up to Eugene, OR and I've lived here ever since. I went to pastry school for college and worked at several bakeries once I moved back home. During the 2020 pandemic, my job got shut down, and I had time to think about my career. Since then, I've decided that I would make the transition to web development. My first attempt wasn't too successful, but near the end of 2021 I was reminded by someone close to me that I should try again. Since then, I've also gotten married and had a child, but somewhere in between and in the late hours of the night I've been learning as much as I can!
